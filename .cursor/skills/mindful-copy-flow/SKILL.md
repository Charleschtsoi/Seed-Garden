# Mindful Copy Flow

## Goal

Write copy that feels gentle, grounded, and trustworthy for emotional support.

## Tone Rules

- Supportive, never judgmental.
- Calm and clear, avoid hype language.
- Short sentences for emotional safety and readability.
- Encourage progress without pressure.

## Section Copy Flow

1. **Hero**: promise one quiet and achievable first step.
2. **User Need**: acknowledge emotional overload without over-pathologizing.
3. **Rituals**: present simple choices (`坐`, `錄`, `聞`, `研`).
4. **Meditation**: emphasize ease of starting and companionship.
5. **Insights**: frame data as reflection, not performance.
6. **Final CTA**: invite gentle continuation, not urgency.

## Do / Avoid

- Do: "Begin with one quiet moment."
- Do: "Notice patterns with kindness."
- Avoid: "Boost productivity now."
- Avoid: "Never miss a streak."

## Copy QA

- [ ] Fits the visual calm of the design.
- [ ] Works in both mobile and desktop section lengths.
- [ ] No fear-driven or pressuring language.
- [ ] CTA text is invitational and specific.
