# Meditation UI Polish

## Goal

Keep Seed Garden visually calm, emotionally safe, and easy to scan.

## Design Principles

- Calm over intensity: avoid saturated colors and sharp contrasts.
- Breathing room: prioritize generous spacing and clean section rhythm.
- Gentle hierarchy: clear headings with soft supporting text.
- Consistent surfaces: keep card/button radius and border language coherent.
- Cultural respect: preserve Chinese Zen references and bilingual balance.

## Practical Rules

- Prefer warm neutral background with one primary accent (`gold` family).
- Keep body text high-legibility but not stark black where possible.
- Avoid crowded icon clusters; use limited visual anchors per section.
- Use soft hover/focus states; no flashy transitions.
- Keep CTA language invitational ("Begin", "Explore", "Try quietly").

## Polish Checklist

- [ ] Typography scales smoothly with `clamp()` where appropriate.
- [ ] Sections feel balanced on both mobile and desktop.
- [ ] Buttons/chips are visually consistent.
- [ ] No component appears louder than the primary CTA.
- [ ] Overall mood matches meditation and emotional support context.
