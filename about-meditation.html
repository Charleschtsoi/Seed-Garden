About meditation

What is meditation

Meditation is a simple practice of returning attention to the present moment. You do not need to empty the mind. You only notice, soften, and return.

Why shall we do meditation

A few quiet minutes can help the body settle and make emotions easier to observe with kindness.

How to do meditation

Sit comfortably, choose a gentle length, and follow the breath. When attention wanders, begin again without blame.

Begin with one quiet moment. Learning can stay light, practical, and close to the body.
